PickerView

public struct PickerView<T> : View

Wrapper of UIPickerView.

A view that uses a spinning-wheel or slot-machine metaphor to show one or more sets of values.

 PickerView(["1","2","3","4","5"]){item in
       Text(item)
  }
  .width(100)
  .height(200)
  .backgroundColor(.yellow)
